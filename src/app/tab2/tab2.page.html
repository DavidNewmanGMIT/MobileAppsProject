This weeks #1 The Nintendo Switch!

Switch is a game console developed by Nintendo, released worldwide, It is a hybrid console that can be used as a home console and portable console like a gameboy. It uses wireless Joy-Con controllers, motion sensing, and tactile feedback, can attach to both sides of the console to support handheld-style play. The Nintendo Switch's software supports online gaming through Internet connectivity, as well as local wireless ad hoc connectivity with other consoles. Nintendo Switch games and software are available on both physical flash-based ROM cartridges and digital distribution via Nintendo eShop; the system has no region lockout. A handheld-focused revision of the system, called the Nintendo Switch Lite, was released on September 20, 2019.
